A Crowdsourced Spiritual Text

The goal is for a large number of people to collectively write a spiritual, secular text that is accessible and useful to other humans as they go about their lives.

It ought to offer advice and companionship on all the major themes we all encounter on this Earth: love, grief, work, family, finance, governance, health, death. All of it.

Through life we all go through ups and downs, and we come away from those experiences knowing a little more than we did before. This text is about distilling our collective knowledge about how best to live a wonderful life into a single, coherent whole.
